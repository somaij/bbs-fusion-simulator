Blitz is a fast, multi-hit Physical command in Kingdom Hearts: Birth by Sleep. It's ideal for chaining into combo finishers and dealing consistent damage to single targets or bosses. Here’s how to unlock it easily through Command Melding.

Recommended Crystals for Blitz

Using a Pulsing Crystal can give offensive damage boosting abilities like Finish Boost, while a Wellspring Crystal offers support abilities such as Combo Plus or Air Combo Plus. Level up Blitz fully to permanently keep its attached ability!

Why Use Blitz?

Blitz deals multiple hits quickly and can lead into finishers, making it useful for single enemies and bosses. It's also a key ingredient for melding Sonic Blade, Ars Arcanum, Triple Blizzaga/Firaga, Magic Hour and Meteor Crash.
