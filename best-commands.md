<h1>Most Popular Commands in KH BBS</h1>
<p>These are the commands that matter most in <strong>Kingdom Hearts: Birth by Sleep</strong> — the ones worth planning your melds around. Each entry below includes the full recipe chain, the best crystal to pair with it, and why it's worth the effort. All four commands listed here are available to <strong>Terra, Ventus, and Aqua</strong>.</p>

<h2>Curaga — The Essential Heal</h2>
<p>Curaga is the strongest healing command in the game. It restores all or nearly all of your HP in one use and scales well into the late game. Every character should have at least one — ideally two — in their deck as soon as it's available.</p>

<h2>Sonic Blade — Best Ranged Physical</h2>
<p>Sonic Blade fires a rapid series of ranged physical strikes that close distance instantly and deal reliable damage without putting you in harm's way. It works against all enemy types, stays useful throughout the entire game, and the right crystal makes it one of the most efficient routes to <strong>Second Chance</strong>.</p>

Blitz + Air Slide with Pulsing is one of the cleanest ability-farming recipes in BBS. Slot Edge is purchased from shops; Air Slide is an action command unlocked through leveling.</p>

<h2>Stopga — Best Crowd Control</h2>
<p>Stopga freezes every enemy on screen simultaneously, giving you free hits with zero risk. It trivializes large groups and staggered boss phases alike. The Stop chain is entirely self-contained — you only need the base Stop command, which is available from the first world's shop.</p>

Both are available to all three characters.</p>

<h2>Mine Shield, Mine Square, and Seeker Mine</h2>
<p>Mine commands place explosive traps on the battlefield. Mine Shield hovers near the player and blocks incoming hits while dealing contact damage; Mine Square drops a stationary minefield. Both are useful independently, and combining them into <strong>Seeker Mine</strong> creates homing projectiles that track down enemies — one of the best tools for mobile bosses.</p>

<h2>Geo Impact, Quake, and Meteor — Terra</h2>
<p>Terra's Geo chain is his most powerful offensive sequence. <strong>Geo Impact</strong> is a massive ground slam that sends out shockwaves; <strong>Quake</strong> causes earthquakes that hit multiple times across the battlefield; and <strong>Meteor</strong> calls down a barrage of space rocks for some of the highest single-hit damage in the game. All three are Terra-exclusive.</p>
<p>The chain runs through <strong>Brutal Blast</strong>, which is also Terra-only. You'll need at least three copies of Brutal Blast — two to make Geo Impact, and one more for Quake.</p>

<h2>Character-Specific Highlights</h2>
<p>Some of the best commands in BBS are locked to specific characters. Here are the standouts worth targeting:</p>
<ul>

    <li><strong>Terra — Chaos Blade:</strong> Terra's most powerful physical command. Meld <strong>Dark Haze + Sonic Blade</strong> (90%) — another reason to build Sonic Blade early.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Tips for Efficient Melding</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Level each command to max before melding — the passive ability unlocks permanently on the leveled copy, so you keep it even after the command is consumed.</li>
    <li>Pulsing Crystals are your priority early: they give <strong>Second Chance</strong> and <strong>Leaf Bracer</strong> on the most accessible recipes.</li>
    <li>Wellspring Crystals give <strong>Once More</strong> and <strong>Combo Plus</strong> — stock them for mid and late-game melds.</li>
    <li>Use the <strong>Find Recipes mode</strong> on the calculator to reverse-search any command you want to build.</li>
</ul>
